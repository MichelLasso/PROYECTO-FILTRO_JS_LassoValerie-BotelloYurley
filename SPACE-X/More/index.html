SPACE X
★ Space Exploration Technologies Corp. known as SpaceX, is an American aerospace manufacturing and space transportation services company based in Hawthorne, California. It was founded in 2002 by Elon Musk with the goal of reducing the costs of space travel to facilitate the colonization of Mars. 456 SpaceX has developed several launch vehicles, the Starlink constellation, the Dragon cargo spacecraft and carried astronauts to the International Space Station on Dragon 2.
